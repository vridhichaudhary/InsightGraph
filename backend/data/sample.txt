Llama 3 is an open-source model developed by Meta. Its 70B parameter version scores 82.0 on MMLU, 81.7 on HumanEval, 93.0 on GSM8K, and 88.0 on HellaSwag.

GPT-4o is a proprietary flagship model by OpenAI. It scores 88.7 on MMLU, 90.2 on HumanEval, 97.1 on GSM8K, and 95.3 on HellaSwag.

In 2019, Transformer models averaged around 1.5 billion parameters. By 2020, GPT-3 reached 175 billion. By 2022, models scaled to 540 billion (PaLM). By 2024, some dense models approached 1.8 trillion parameters.
